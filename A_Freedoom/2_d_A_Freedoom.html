2_A_Freedoom
You 'll be exploring a series of levels , in each one trying to find the way to the exit .

3_A_Freedoom
An assortment of monsters will try to stop you , and you 'll need to use weapons to defend yourself .

4_A_Freedoom
Portions of the levels may be inaccessible until you find a particular key , or find a switch to open a locked door .

5_A_Freedoom
This gives a puzzle element to the game in addition to the action .
