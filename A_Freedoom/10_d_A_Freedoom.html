16_A_Freedoom
Picking something up is a simple matter of walking over it -- an on-screen message and a brief flash of the screen indicate that you 've done so successfully .

17_A_Freedoom
If you do n't pick it up , it 's likely you do n't need it right now ( for example , you ca n't pick up a health pack when you already have 100 % health ) .
