44_A_Freedoom
The primary instinct of all monsters is to move towards you .

45_A_Freedoom
Circle around the crowd continually -- this encourages them to cluster in a single spot that 's easier for you to target .

46_A_Freedoom
It also encourages monster in-fighting ; if done effectively , they 'll spend their energy killing each other and you 'll save on ammunition .
