6_A_Freedoom
By default , the keyboard cursor keys will move forward and backward , and turn left and right .

7_A_Freedoom
The Control key fires the current weapon , and the spacebar will open doors and activate switches .

8_A_Freedoom
All source ports allow you to rebind these keys to a setup that you find more comfortable .
