8_A_Planetoid
If you do n't rescue a life - form before the raider carrying him reaches the top of the screen , the raider will mutate .

9_A_Planetoid
You will then have to contend with a mutant , while also being attacked by bombers , spores and megacytes ; all of which are to be avoided at all costs and killed before going on to the next wave .
