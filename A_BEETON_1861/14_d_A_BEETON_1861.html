27_A_BEETON_1861
Supposing eggs ( the cheapest ) to be used , — Draw a gallon or so of the wine , and mix one quart of it with the whites of four eggs , by stirring it with a whisk ; afterwards , when thoroughly mixed , pour it back into the cask through the bunghole , and stir up the whole cask , in a rotatory direction , with a clean split stick inserted through the bunghole .     [2166-6]
