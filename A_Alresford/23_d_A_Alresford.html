27_A_Alresford
At first glance , Alresford seemed to lack theatre , but Guy Ingram and Monica Griffin at the Hobby Horse enrolled me in Ropley Dramatic Society where there was energy and talent , and I soon discovered plays were performed regularly by good amateur groups in Hampshire village halls .
