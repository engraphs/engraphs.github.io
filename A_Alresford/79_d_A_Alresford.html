85_A_Alresford
My lasting impression is a series of moments illustrating the sheer loveliness of the Hampshire countryside ; deer at dusk on the edge of Bramdean Common ; the impenetrable , neglected roses in full bloom around the ruins of Worthington Grange , before it was tidied up and made safe ; masses of butterflies on Abbotstone Down , waterfowl on Alresford Pond watched from the garden of the Globe , in town , flourishing hanging baskets in summer and , in dark December evenings , the Christmas trees over every shop .
