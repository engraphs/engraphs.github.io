4_A_NINJAL_mission
The National Institute for Japanese Language and Linguistics ( NINJAL ) is now in the sixth and final year of the period of the Second Medium-Term that began with its conversion to an inter-university research institute under the umbrella of the National Institutes for the Humanities ( NIHU ) .

5_A_NINJAL_mission
NIHU provides a new framework for comprehensive humanities research in areas such as history , culture , folklore , literature , and human interaction with the natural environment .
