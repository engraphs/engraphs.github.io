3_A_stick
The use of stick allows you to define your application by means of Obey files called !Select , !Adjust and !Drag , which are triggered when these actions take place .

4_A_stick
These Obey files ( or TaskObey files in appropriate circumstances ) may typically execute non - wimp programs in other languages .

5_A_stick
In other words , stick creates a wimp - task that handles only clicks on the iconbar ( including Menu ) or drags to the iconbar , and nothing else , pushing out to !Select , !Adjust and !Drag , as far as they exist , the responsibility for how the computer should react .
