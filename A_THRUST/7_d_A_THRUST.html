11_A_THRUST
Each planet has a reactor providing power for the limpet guns .

12_A_THRUST
Shooting the reactor will damage it , and the guns will cease firing until power can be restored .

13_A_THRUST
The more damage done , the longer this will take .

14_A_THRUST
If you shoot the reactor repeatedly , it will become critically damaged .

15_A_THRUST
You will then have a limited amount of time to escape before the reactor explodes and the planet is destroyed .

16_A_THRUST
If this happens , and you have not collected the pod , then the mission has failed .

17_A_THRUST
If you retrieve the pod , send the reactor into its critical phase and leave the planet safely , you receive an extra bonus of 2000 points .
