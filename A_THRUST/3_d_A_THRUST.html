5_A_THRUST
To do so you must locate the pod , hover just above it , activate your starship 's tractor beam and thrust away from the pod .

6_A_THRUST
Once the beam has locked onto the pod , you can deactivate the tractor beam .

7_A_THRUST
You must then carry the pod away from the planet .
