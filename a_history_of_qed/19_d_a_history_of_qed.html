36_a_history_of_qed
The “ standard Unix editor ” ed was first written by Ken Thompson for the PDP .

37_a_history_of_qed
It kept the basic text - line orientation , but radically simplified the regular expressions to include only the operator : no alternation , no parentheses .

38_a_history_of_qed
Where my QED embraced much of the context - free languages , this version could not even express all regular languages .

39_a_history_of_qed
It was not much of a loss .

40_a_history_of_qed
Similarly , Ken 's Unix ed ditched the notion of multiple buffers and of execution of buffers .
