15_a_history_of_qed
CTSS was used at Bell Labs as part of our participation in the Multics project , and as Ken began to use Multics , he wrote yet another version of QED for that system .

16_a_history_of_qed
This one was in BCPL , and instead of compiling to machine code , regular expressions were represented as trees that were interpreted by the editor .
