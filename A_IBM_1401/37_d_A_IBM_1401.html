50_A_IBM_1401
Tape Utilities

51_A_IBM_1401
These are generalized instructions , particularly useful to 1401 customers who also use larger data processing systems .

52_A_IBM_1401
They facilitate the transfer of data between IBM cards , magnetic tapes , and printers .

53_A_IBM_1401
They also provide for some 1401 processing while the transfer of data is taking place .
