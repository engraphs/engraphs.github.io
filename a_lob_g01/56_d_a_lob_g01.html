The only concession made in view of the objections to the first two poll_-_taxes was the suggestion that the rich should help the poor — but this was only a pious hope because no machinery was provided for carrying it into effect , and a subordinate clause went far to nullify what small effects it had — no man and wife together were to pay more than twenty shillings , a restriction which applied to the generous rich as well as to the mean .
