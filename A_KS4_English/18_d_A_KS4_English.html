25_A_KS4_English
Pupils should be taught to control their speaking and writing consciously , understand why sentences are constructed as they are and to use Standard English .     [PoS_FINAL_170714]

26_A_KS4_English
They should understand and use age-appropriate vocabulary , including linguistic and literary terminology , for discussing their reading , writing and spoken language .     [PoS_FINAL_170714]

27_A_KS4_English
This involves consolidation , practice and discussion of language .     [PoS_FINAL_170714]
