1Inauguration of free zone in Dubai for e - commerce
2Dubai 10-28 (FP) -
3Dubai's Crown Prince Sheikh Mohamed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum inaugurated a free zone for e - commerce today, called Dubai Internet City.
4The preliminary stages of the project, the only one of its kind according to its designers, are estimated at <dollar> 200 million.

7He said companies engaged in e - commerce would be able to set up offices, employ staff and own equipment in the open zone, including fully - owned foreign companies.

9The inauguration of Dubai Internet City coincides with the opening of an annual IT show in Dubai, the Gulf Information Technology Exhibition (Gitex), the biggest in the Middle East.
