22_A_driver_handbook
You must drive slower when there is heavy traffic or bad weather .

23_A_driver_handbook
However , if you block the normal and reasonable movement of traffic by driving too slowly , you may be given a ticket .

24_A_driver_handbook
If you choose to drive slower than other traffic , do not drive in the Number 1 ( fast ) lane .
