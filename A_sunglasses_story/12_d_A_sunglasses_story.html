16_A_sunglasses_story
Once there was a person travelling from your country to the other country .

17_A_sunglasses_story
She was smart and understood that if she wanted to learn about the other country and the other people she had to get a pair of blue glasses to be able to “ see ” .

18_A_sunglasses_story
When she arrived in the other country she made sure she got a pair of blue glasses .

19_A_sunglasses_story
She stayed for three months and felt that she really learned a lot about the other people 's values , faith and ideas .

20_A_sunglasses_story
She could really “ see ” with the help of the new blue glasses .

21_A_sunglasses_story
When she arrived home in her own country she became an expert on the other country and proudly told a lot of interested listeners that the other culture was green .
