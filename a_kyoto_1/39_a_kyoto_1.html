39_a_kyoto_1
Reflecting Sesshu's popularity, a Japanese puppet drama / Kabuki work entitled “The Gion Religious Festival” was created. The drama was first performed in January 1758. The scene of Kinkaku-ji Temple, where Sesshu's granddaughter, Yukihime, takes an active role, is famous and similar works were performed.
