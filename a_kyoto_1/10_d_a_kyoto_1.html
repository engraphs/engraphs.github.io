36_a_kyoto_1
Sesshu started to be deified in the Edo period .     [BDS_00001;36]

37_a_kyoto_1
It is thought to be because the Kano School that ruled painting circles at the time venerated him as a master , and feudal lords wanted to have Sesshu 's artwork .     [BDS_00001;37]

38_a_kyoto_1
It is said that this is why the number of artworks attributed to Sesshu increased .     [BDS_00001;38]

39_a_kyoto_1
Reflecting Sesshu 's popularity , a Japanese puppet drama / Kabuki work entitled “ The Gion Religious Festival ” was created . The drama was first performed in January 1758 . The scene of Kinkaku_-_ji Temple , where Sesshu 's granddaughter , Yukihime , takes an active role , is famous and similar works were performed .     [BDS_00001;39]

40_a_kyoto_1
Today , Sesshu who gave birth to one of Japanese cultures is a leading historical figure of this country .     [BDS_00001;42]
