121_a_hedgehog
The hedgehog is of course an important environmental indicator , with its habitat , its ability to occupy 30 hectares of land , and its particular relationship to the hibernaculum , by which I mean the hedgehog 's ability , almost uniquely among animals in the United Kingdom , to go into a state of genuine hibernation .
