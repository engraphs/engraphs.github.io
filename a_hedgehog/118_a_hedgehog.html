118_a_hedgehog
This strange animal was known, of course, in Scotland, Wales and Ireland originally in Gaelic as that demonic creature, that horrid creature, and is the hedgehog celebrated by Shakespeare: “Thorny hedgehogs, be not seen... Come not near our faerie queen”, and famously of course in “Richard III” there is that great moment when Gloucester is referred to as a hedgehog.
