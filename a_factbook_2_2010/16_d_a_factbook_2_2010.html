71_a_factbook_2_2010
Canon law is not a divine law as such because it is not found in revelation .

72_a_factbook_2_2010
It is viewed instead as human law inspired by the word of God and applying the demands of that revelation to the actual situation of the church .

73_a_factbook_2_2010
Canon law regulates the internal ordering of the Roman Catholic Church , the Eastern Orthodox Church , and the Anglican Communion .
