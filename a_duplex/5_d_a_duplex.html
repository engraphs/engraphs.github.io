7_a_duplex
First shown at the tech giant 's developers ' conference in May , Duplex has become one of the most talked about demos of the year so far .

8_a_duplex
At that event , we heard the robot voice book a table at a restaurant by calling it up , speaking to a human member of staff , and confirming the details .

9_a_duplex
What startled people was that the voice , while synthesised , was incredibly lifelike - even adding its own “ uh_-_huh ” and “ mm ” to the flow of conversation .

10_a_duplex
Indeed , the recipient of the call may have no idea they are talking to a robot .

11_a_duplex
That 's kind of the point , but that did n't stop an enormous debate about the rights and wrongs of such an exchange .

12_a_duplex
There was also cynicism about the demo itself .
