1_a_joseph_byers
Private Joseph Byers was the first Kitchener volunteer to be executed .

2_a_joseph_byers
He was 17 and under age when he enlisted in the 1st Royal Scots Fusiliers in November 1914 , and was sent to France with two weeks training .

3_a_joseph_byers
By January 1915 , his inexperience and the horrors he witnessed caused him to go absent without leave with another private , Andrew Evans .
