1Jimmy Carter , Voyager Spacecraft Statement by the President .
2July 29 , 1977
3Note_: The statement has been placed in a National Aeronautics and Space Administration Voyager spacecraft which is scheduled to be launched August 20 .
4The statement is recorded in electronic impulses which can be converted into printed words .
5This Voyager spacecraft was constructed by the United States of America . We are a community of 240 million human beings among the more than 4 billion who inhabit the planet Earth .
6We human beings are still divided into nation states , but these states are rapidly becoming a single global civilization . We cast this message into the cosmos . It is likely to survive a billion years into our future , when our civilization is profoundly altered and the surface of the Earth may be vastly changed .
7Of the 200 billion stars in the Milky Way galaxy , some -- perhaps many -- may have inhabited planets and spacefaring civilizations .
8If one such civilization intercepts Voyager and can understand these recorded contents , here is our message :
9This is a present from a small distant world , a token of our sounds , our science , our images , our music , our thoughts , and our feelings . We are attempting to survive our time so we may live into yours . We hope someday , having solved the problems we face , to join a community of galactic civilizations . This record represents our hope and our determination , and our good will in a vast and awesome universe .
