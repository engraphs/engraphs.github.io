1My oldest pet is a mongrel dog called Mickey.
2Mickey's body is black but he has a white stomach, white feet, a white streak down his nose, and a white fluffet on the end of his tail.
3Mickey was bought when he was only a puppy of three weeks.
4Being blind at first Mickey always kept bumping into objects that were in his path but as his eyes formed he began to see.
5What I thought was a most humorous action by Mickey was when he discovered the staircase.
6Mickey clambered up in his puppy ways and when arriving on the top step he realised the height and his terror would not let him go down.
7He whimpered continually until someone came up the stairs and ‘rescued’ him.
8Nowadays he is an extremely intelligent dog.
9My father is on shift work and Mickey can tell which shift he is on.
10When my father is on night shift he comes home in the morning and under all circumstances Mickey will welcome him.
11He listens for the car door and then waits for dad.
12He certainly is a very intelligent pet to own.
