121_A_Hedgehog
The hedgehog is of course an important environmental indicator , with its habitat , its ability to occupy 30 hectares of land , and its particular relationship to the hibernaculum , by which I mean the hedgehog 's ability , almost uniquely among animals in the United Kingdom , to go into a state of genuine hibernation .     [2015-11-10;speaker=Stewart]

122_A_Hedgehog
Its heartbeat goes from 240 a minute to only two a minute for six months a year .     [2015-11-10;speaker=Stewart]

123_A_Hedgehog
It has a particular diet -- a focus on grubs and beetles .     [2015-11-10;speaker=Stewart]
