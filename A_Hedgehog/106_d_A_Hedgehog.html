118_A_Hedgehog
This strange animal was known , of course , in Scotland , Wales and Ireland originally in Gaelic as that demonic creature , that horrid creature , and is the hedgehog celebrated by Shakespeare : “ Thorny hedgehogs , be not seen ... Come not near our faerie queen ” , and famously of course in “ Richard III ” there is that great moment when Gloucester is referred to as a hedgehog .     [2015-11-10;speaker=Stewart]
