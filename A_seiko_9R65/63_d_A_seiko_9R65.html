80_A_seiko_9R65
○ When starting to use a stopped watch , it is recommended that you turn the crown to wind the mainspring .

81_A_seiko_9R65
To wind the mainspring , turn the crown at the normal position clockwise ( 12 o'clock direction ) slowly .

82_A_seiko_9R65
If you turn the crown counterclockwise ( 6 o'clock direction ) , it will turn free .

83_A_seiko_9R65
Five full rotations of the crown will provide the power to run the watch for approximately ten hours .
