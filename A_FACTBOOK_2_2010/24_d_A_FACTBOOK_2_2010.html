101_A_FACTBOOK_2_2010
US law draws its authority from four sources : constitutional law , statutory law , administrative regulations , and case law .

102_A_FACTBOOK_2_2010
Constitutional law is based on the US Constitution and serves as the supreme federal law .

103_A_FACTBOOK_2_2010
Taken together with those of the state constitutions , these documents outline the general structure of the federal and state governments and provide the rules and limits of power .
