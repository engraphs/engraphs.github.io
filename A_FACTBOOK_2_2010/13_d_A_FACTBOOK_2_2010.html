Mixed Law - Also referred to as pluralistic law , mixed law consists of elements of some or all of the other main types of legal systems - civil , common , customary , and religious .

58_A_FACTBOOK_2_2010
The mixed legal systems of a number of countries came about when colonial powers overlaid their own legal systems upon colonized regions but retained elements of the colonies ' existing legal systems .
