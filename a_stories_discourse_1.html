1Pointing to seashells and bones scattered at his feet, he said to us, “Let's take these home as well.”
2Everyone picked them up, and put them in a bag that was lying around.
3That big bag was full of them, but he somehow crammed it into his rucksack.

5“Teacher! Are you OK?”; we asked him in surprised voices.
