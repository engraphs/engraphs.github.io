30_A_KYOTO_1
There is an interesting episode about Sesshu .     [BDS_00001;30]

31_A_KYOTO_1
A young Sesshu who entered Hofuku-ji Temple only liked pictures and did not want to read sutras ; so the monks of the temple tied him to a pillar in the building enshrining a Buddhist statue .     [BDS_00001;31]

32_A_KYOTO_1
However , when the monks saw that he drew a rat with his tears that fell on the floor by using his big toe , they were impressed so they allowed him to draw pictures .     [BDS_00001;32]

33_A_KYOTO_1
This is thought to be the most well known story about Sesshu .     [BDS_00001;33]
