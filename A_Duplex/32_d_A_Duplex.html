53_A_Duplex
Once someone prompts a Duplex call to find out , whatever answer the system receives from the human will instantly be placed into Google 's database , viewable to all .

54_A_Duplex
Google sees that as a win-win .

55_A_Duplex
It gets up-to-date information - without any legwork or expense on its part - and the restaurant , in theory , only takes one “ are you open tomorrow ? ” call .
