75_A_ANC_HistoryOfGreece
As the Byzantine Empire weakened at the end of the first millennium , Crusader forces were sent from Western Europe to counter the Muslim forces and retake Jerusalem for the Christian faith .

76_A_ANC_HistoryOfGreece
Unfortunately , their zeal was not matched by their discrimination .

77_A_ANC_HistoryOfGreece
The crusaders swept through the land of Byzantium slaughtering Christians as well as Muslims , civilians as well as soldiers .

78_A_ANC_HistoryOfGreece
Constantinople was taken by Crusader forces in 1204 , and they stripped the city of many of its finest treasures <ndash> which now grace the public buildings of Venice <ndash> although a large consignment of books and manuscripts was transferred to the monastery at Patmos before the city fell .
