47_A_history_of_qed
The current local state of the QED line of descent is exemplified by sam , by Rob Pike ( see his “ The Text Editor sam , ” in Software -- Practice and Experience 17 # 11 , Nov. 1987 ) .

48_A_history_of_qed
It 's available for FTP in both Unix and Windows versions ; search for “ sam editor ” on netlib .

49_A_history_of_qed
Sam offers cut - and - paste , mouse - and - menu editing on its text windows together with a command window , the language of which is drawn from the QED line of descent .
