42_A_history_of_qed
Subsequent developments along this line include vi , done mostly by Bill Joy at UC Berkeley .

43_A_history_of_qed
According to George Coulouris , vi was based on ( or at least inspired by ) the em editor done at Queen Mary College in London .

44_A_history_of_qed
The advance here was essentially to adapt the command - set of ed to a screen - editor format ; instead of doing things on ( possibly virtual ) paper on a typewriter , vi keeps a current view of a piece of a document on the screen , while commands are typed on the bottom line .
