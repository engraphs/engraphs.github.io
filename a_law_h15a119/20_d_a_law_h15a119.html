( 5 ) The term “ retained personal data ” as used in this Act shall mean such personal data over which a business operator handling personal information has the authority to disclose , to correct , add or delete the content , to discontinue its utilization , to erase , and to discontinue its provision to a third party , excluding the data which is specified by a Cabinet Order as harming public or other interests if its presence or absence is known and the data which will be erased within a period of no longer than one year that is specified by a Cabinet Order .     [17_1_2_5]
