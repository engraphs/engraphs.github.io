(1) When a business operator handling personal information is requested by a person to correct, add, or delete such retained personal data as may lead to the identification of the person on the ground that the retained personal data is contrary to the fact, the business operator shall, except in cases in which special procedures are prescribed by any other laws and regulations for such correction, addition, or deletion, make a necessary investigation without delay within the scope necessary for the achievement of the Purpose of Utilization and, on the basis of the results, correct, add, or delete the retained personal data.
