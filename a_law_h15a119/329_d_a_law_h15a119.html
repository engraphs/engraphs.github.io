329_a_law_h15a119
Where a person has given consent to the handling of his or her personal information prior to enforcement of this Act , and where the consent is equivalent to the consent that allows the personal data to be provided to a third party under paragraph ( 1 ) of Article 23 , then it shall be deemed that there is such consent as is prescribed in the same paragraph .     [251_Supp_3]
