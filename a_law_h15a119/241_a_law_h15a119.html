241_a_law_h15a119
(1) When an authorized personal information protection organization is requested by a person, etc., to solve a complaint about the handling of personal information by a target business operator, corresponding to the request, the organization shall give the person, etc. necessary advice, investigate the circumstances pertaining to the complaint and request the target business operator to solve the complaint promptly by notifying the target business operator of the content of the complaint.
