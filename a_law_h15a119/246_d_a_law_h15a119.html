( 1 ) In order to ensure the proper handling of personal information by its target entities , each authorized personal information protection organization shall endeavor to draw up and publicly announce guidelines ( hereinafter referred to as “ personal information protection guidelines ” ) in conformity with the purport of the provisions of this Act , concerning the specification of the Purpose of Utilization , security control measures , procedures for complying with individuals ' requests , and other matters .     [194_4_43_1]
