(1) When a business operator handling personal information has violated any of the provisions of Article 16 to Article 18, Article 20 to Article 27, or paragraph (2) of Article 30, the competent Minister may recommend that the business operator handling personal information cease the violation and take other necessary measures to correct the violation when a competent Minister finds it necessary for protecting the rights and interests of individuals.
