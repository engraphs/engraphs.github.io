2_a_kyoto_2
Rinzai Zen Buddhism is one of the Chinese five Houses / seven Schools of Zen ( Rinzai , Igyou , Soto , Unmon , Hogan ) and Gigen RINZAI ( ？ - 867 ) of Tang was its founder .     [BDS_00002;2]

3_a_kyoto_2
He was known as “ RINZAI of Katu ( meaning to help wake up the sleeping mind ) ” and “ General RINZAI ” , and rose to the top of Chinese Zen with his distinctive style .     [BDS_00002;3]

4_a_kyoto_2
With its Zen Talks that try to awaken self awareness through study of Koan , it differs from the Silent Zen of the Soto sect , which just does meditation .     [BDS_00002;4]
