337_A_law_h15A119
The provisions of Article 45 shall not apply , for six months after the provision of the same article is enforced , to any business operator actually using the name “ authorized personal information protection organization ” or a name that might be mistaken for it at the time when this Act is enforced .     [256_Supp_6]
