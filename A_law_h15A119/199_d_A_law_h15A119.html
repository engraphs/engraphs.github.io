199_A_law_h15A119
( 2 ) In light of the purport of the provision of the preceding paragraph , with respect to the act of a business operator handling personal information to provide an individual or business operator mentioned in each item of paragraph ( 1 ) of Article 50 ( limited to cases in which the personal information is handled for a purpose as respectively provided in each of such items ) with personal information , the competent Minister shall not exercise its authority .     [156_4_35_2]
