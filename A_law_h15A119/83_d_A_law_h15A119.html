83_A_law_h15A119
( 1 ) A business operator handling personal information shall not handle personal information about a person , without obtaining the prior consent of the person , beyond the scope necessary for the achievement of the Purpose of Utilization specified pursuant to the provision of the preceding article .     [66_4_16_1]
