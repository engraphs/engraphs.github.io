( 3 ) Notwithstanding the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs , where a business operator handling personal information has violated any of the provisions of Article 16 , Article 17 , Articles 20 to 22 , or paragraph ( 1 ) of Article 23 , and when the competent minister finds it necessary to take measures urgently as there is the fact of serious infringement of the rights and interests of individuals , the competent minister may order the business operator handling personal information to cease the violation and take other necessary measures to rectify the violation .     [153_4_34_3]
