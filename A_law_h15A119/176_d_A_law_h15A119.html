176_A_law_h15A119
( 4 ) When a business operator determine the procedures for meeting requests for disclosure and others under the provisions of the preceding three paragraphs , the business operator shall take into consideration that the procedures will not impose excessively heavy burden on the persons making requests for disclosure and others .     [139_4_29_4]
