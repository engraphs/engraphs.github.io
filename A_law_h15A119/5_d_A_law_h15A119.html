5_A_law_h15A119
The purpose of this Act is to protect the rights and interests of individuals while taking consideration of the usefulness of personal information , in view of a remarkable increase in the utilization of personal information due to development of the advanced information and communications society , by clarifying the responsibilities of the State and local governments , etc. with laying down basic principle , establishment of a basic policy by the Government and the matters to serve as a basis for other measures on the protection of personal information , and by prescribing the duties to be observed by entities handling personal information , etc. , regarding the proper handling of personal information .     [4_1_1]
