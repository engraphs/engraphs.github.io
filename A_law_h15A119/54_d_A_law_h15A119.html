54_A_law_h15A119
In order to support the measures for the protection of personal information formulated or implemented by local governments and the activities performed by citizens , entities , and others to ensure the proper handling of personal information , the State shall provide information , formulate guidelines to ensure the appropriate and effective implementation of measures to be taken by entities and others , and take any other necessary measures .     [45_3_8]
