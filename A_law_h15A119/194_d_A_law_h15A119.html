194_A_law_h15A119
( 2 ) Where a business operator handling personal information having received a recommendation under the provision of the preceding paragraph does not take the recommended measures without justifiable ground , and when the competent minister finds that the serious infringement on the rights and interests of individuals is imminent , the competent minister may order the business operator handling personal information to take the recommended measures .     [152_4_34_2]
