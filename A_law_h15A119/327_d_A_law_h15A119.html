327_A_law_h15A119
Where a person has given consent to the handling of his or her personal information prior to enforcement of this Act , and where the consent is equivalent to the consent that allows the personal information to be handled for a purpose other than the Purpose of Utilization specified under paragraph ( 1 ) of Article 15 , then it shall be deemed that there is such consent as is prescribed in paragraph ( 1 ) or ( 2 ) of Article 16 .     [250_Supp_2]
