233_A_law_h15A119
( 1 ) When a business operator authorized under paragraph ( 1 ) of Article 37 ( hereinafter referred to as “ authorized personal information protection organization ” ) intends to abolish the business pertaining to the authorization ( hereinafter referred to as “ authorized business ” ) , it shall notify the competent minister of that effect in advance as prescribed by a Cabinet Order .     [184_4_40_1]
