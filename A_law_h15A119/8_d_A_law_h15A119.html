8_A_law_h15A119
( 1 ) The term “ personal information ” as used in this Act shall mean information about a living individual which can identify the specific individual by name , date of birth or other description contained in such information ( including such information as will allow easy reference to other information and will thereby enable the identification of the specific individual ) .     [6_1_2_1]
