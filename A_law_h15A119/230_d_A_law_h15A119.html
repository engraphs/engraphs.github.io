( iii ) When the applicant conducts any business other than the businesses mentioned in the items of paragraph ( 1 ) of Article 37 , by conducting the business , the applicant shall not be likely to impede the fair execution of the businesses mentioned in the same items of the same paragraph .     [182_4_39_3iii]
