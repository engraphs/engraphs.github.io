168_A_law_h15A119
When a business operator handling personal information notifies a person requesting the business operator to take certain measures pursuant to the provisions of paragraph ( 3 ) of Article 24 , paragraph ( 2 ) of Article 25 , paragraph ( 2 ) of Article 26 , or paragraph ( 3 ) of the preceding article that the business operator will not take all or part of the measures or that the business operator will take different measures , the business operator shall endeavor to explain the reasons .     [134_4_28]
