334_A_law_h15A119
If an individual has been notified , prior to enforcement of this Act , of the matters that shall be notified to the individual or be put in a readily accessible condition for the individual under item ( iii ) of paragraph ( 4 ) of Article 23 , then it shall be deemed that the notice concerned has been given under the provision of the same paragraph .     [254_Supp_5]
