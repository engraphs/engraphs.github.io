63_a_factbook_1_2010
Schistosomiasis - caused by parasitic trematode flatworm Schistosoma ;

64_a_factbook_1_2010
fresh water snails act as intermediate host and release larval form of parasite that penetrates the skin of people exposed to contaminated water ;

65_a_factbook_1_2010
worms mature and reproduce in the blood vessels , liver , kidneys , and intestines releasing eggs , which become trapped in tissues triggering an immune response ;

66_a_factbook_1_2010
may manifest as either urinary or intestinal disease resulting in decreased work or learning capacity ;

67_a_factbook_1_2010
mortality , while generally low , may occur in advanced cases usually due to bladder cancer ;

68_a_factbook_1_2010
endemic in 74 developing countries with 80 % of infected people living in sub_-_Saharan Africa ;

69_a_factbook_1_2010
humans act as the reservoir for this parasite .
