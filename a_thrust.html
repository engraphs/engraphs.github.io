1The Mission:
2The Resistance is about to launch a major offensive against the Empire.
3In preparation for this, they have captured several starships, but they lack the essential power sources for these formidable craft, Klyston Pods.
4You have been commissioned by the Resistance to steal these pods from the Empire's storage panels.
5To do so you must locate the pod, hover just above it, activate your starship's tractor beam and thrust away from the pod.
6Once the beam has locked onto the pod, you can deactivate the tractor beam.
7You must then carry the pod away from the planet.
8You receive a bonus at the end of each successful mission.
9PRESS THE SPACE BAR TO CONTINUE
10The Planet's Reactor:
11Each planet has a reactor providing power for the limpet guns.
12Shooting the reactor will damage it, and the guns will cease firing until power can be restored.
13The more damage done, the longer this will take.
14If you shoot the reactor repeatedly, it will become critically damaged.
15You will then have a limited amount of time to escape before the reactor explodes and the planet is destroyed.
16If this happens, and you have not collected the pod, then the mission has failed.
17If you retrieve the pod, send the reactor into its critical phase and leave the planet safely, you receive an extra bonus of 2000 points.
18Automatic Limpet Guns:
19These guns are the planets' defence system.
20Accurate shooting from your spaceship will destroy the limpet guns.
21Fuel Tanks:
22To collect fuel, hover just above a fuel tank and activate the tractor beam.
23Destroying a limpet gun.... 750 points
24Destroying a fuel tank..... 150 points
25Collecting a fuel tank..... 300 points
26An extra spaceship is awarded for every 10,000 points scored.
